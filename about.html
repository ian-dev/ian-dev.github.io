Philosophy


*Meta: Beyond, after. A prefix that indicates when a concept is analyzed at a higher level.

Metadesign is a methodology, where the design is conceived by its impact on the user and the society beyond its elementary function.

Design with a purpose, honest and unpretentious. Pragmatic and simple. Inspired by nature.

This is Ian.Dev approach to design.
